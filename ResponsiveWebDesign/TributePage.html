Timeline of Annie Easley's Life

  • 1960 Helped with protests for black people to have to right to vote in Alabama (law passed in 1960)
  • 1970 Return to school to recieve a degree in mathematics
  • 1989 Annie Easley retired
  • 2011 Annie Easley died, but continues to be an inspiration today
